Brandon Kaier

Field CTO

Brandon is a founding member and the leader of the Dell | EMC Pathfinder Team.Brandon Kaier has more than two decades of experience in the IT industry as a transformational leader. Kaier is responsible for setting the strategy, defining the service line offerings and capabilities as the Field CTO for the North America. These responsibilities include bringing net new products and solutions to market with a focus on the deployment of solutions enabling analytics and agile application development. Most recently Kaier held positions on Technical Architecture team and Strategy and Direction team for Dell EMC’s Data Lake solutions. Kaier also brings experienced based, EMC IT best practices, Big Data and Transformational strategies to EMC customers. Kaier has a wide range of experiences; most recently, at a global Telecommunications and CRM provider, he led strategic initiatives to create a federated IT ecosystem across twenty three facilities in five countries. Kaier was responsible for all aspects of IT infrastructure from the data center to the desktop along with the associated IT fiduciary responsibilities of the infrastructure. He has faced many of the same staffing, budgeting and tough decisions that are pervasive across the IT industry.
